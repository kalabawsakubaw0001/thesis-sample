About Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib

Our Vision

Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib is a group dedicated to exploring the depths of Filipino cultural narratives through contemporary theatrical practices. We believe in the power of performance to reflect, question, and inspire societal discourse. Our work aims to blend traditional storytelling with innovative staging, making theater accessible and relevant to diverse audiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce compelling theatrical experiences that resonate with the Filipino spirit, foster artistic collaboration, and contribute to the vibrant landscape of Philippine performing arts. We are committed to nurturing emerging talent and providing a platform for critical engagement with our cultural heritage.
