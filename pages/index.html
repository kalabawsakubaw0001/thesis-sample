Welcome to the Journey of Theatrical Production

This website serves as a detailed documentary through the essential stages of a live theatrical performance: Pre-Production, Production (including the live run), and Documentation & Archiving. For aspiring creatives, theater enthusiasts, students, or simply the curious, we present each stage with clarity and in-depth understanding.

Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib aims to showcase the meticulous planning, collaborative efforts, and artistic dedication required to transform a script into a living, breathing stage act. Dive in to discover the passion behind the scenes!
