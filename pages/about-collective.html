About Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib

I. Our Mission and Vision

**Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib** is a dedicated group of students from the Bachelor of Performing Arts, Major in Theater Arts, committed to creating meaningful and impactful stage performances. Our mission is to utilize the powerful medium of live theater to give voice to compelling stories and address significant societal issues, particularly within the rich context of Filipino culture and traditions.

A. Inspiration and Philosophy

The name "Kalabaw sa Kubaw" (Carabao in its Enclosure) symbolizes key values we strive to embody in our artistic endeavors: diligence, resilience, humility, and a deep connection to our cultural roots.
