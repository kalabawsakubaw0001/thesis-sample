About Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib

Our Vision, Mission, and the Dedicated Team Behind the Production.

Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib is a group dedicated to the art of theatrical storytelling, committed to producing compelling and meaningful performances. Our work is driven by a passion for the stage and a belief in the power of theater to connect, provoke, and inspire.

Our Vision

To be a leading voice in contemporary Philippine theater, known for innovative productions that challenge perceptions, ignite dialogue, and celebrate the rich tapestry of human experience.

Our Mission

To create impactful theatrical works that are both artistically excellent and socially relevant. We aim to:

  • Produce High-Quality Performances: Through rigorous training, creative collaboration, and meticulous attention to detail.
  • Foster Artistic Growth: Provide a nurturing environment for emerging and established artists to hone their craft.
  • Engage with the Community: Use theater as a platform to explore contemporary issues and promote cultural understanding.
  • Innovate Theatrical Practices: Experiment with new forms, technologies, and storytelling approaches to keep theater vibrant and accessible.
