About Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektibs

I. Our Mission and Vision

**Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektibs** is a group of students from the Bachelor of Performing Arts, Major in Theater, dedicated to creating meaningful art. Our mission is to use the medium of film and performative arts to give voice to stories and issues important to society, especially within the context of Filipino culture.

A. Inspiration and Philosophy
